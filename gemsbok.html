GEMSBOK

The gemsbok or South African oryx, is a large antelope in the genus Oryx. It is endemic to the dry and barren regions of Botswana,Namibia,South Africa and parts of Zimbabwe, mainly in the Kalahari Desert. The gemsbok is the national animal of Namibia and is featured on the country's coat of arms. The gemsbok is a large antelope with long, straight horns and a distinctive black and white facial pattern. It is well adapted to arid environments and can survive long periods without water.
